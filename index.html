Digital Implant Workflow

1. Prescription & Case Review

Structured review of RX and STL files including implant position, restorative space, screw channel trajectory, and aesthetic limitations before design begins.

2. Restorative-Driven Digital Design

Designed within exocad to prescription. When conflicts arise, direct communication is initiated prior to fabrication to confirm the appropriate restorative path.

3. In-House Milling & Fabrication

Custom abutments, implant restorations, and frameworks milled in-house across dedicated production units.

4. Finishing & Quality Control

Final fit verification and occlusal evaluation prior to release—engineered to minimize chairside adjustment.
